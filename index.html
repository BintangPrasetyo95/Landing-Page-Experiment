RUBY

Ruby is A Fighter/Tank that inherits Spell vamp for all of her skills and has a set of hard crowd control skills that allows her to disrupt the enemy front. Ruby can be played either as Fighter or a Roaming Tank and can easily distrupt enemy skills such as Odette Odette and Pharsa Pharsa. She is also one of the most annoying Crowd Control hero in Mobile Legends due to her multi-stuns and her massive Spell Vamp.
